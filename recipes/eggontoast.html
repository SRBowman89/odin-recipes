Egg on Toast Recipe

Any man worth his salt can make a good fried egg on toast!

It takes much more skill to make the perfect fried egg than many think, that's what I'm telling myself anyway!

ingredients

  • Bread
  • 1 large egg
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a spoonful of butter in the pan on a medium/low heat
  2. Stick the bread in the toaster
  3. Once the butter is bubbling crack your egg in the pan
  4. When the toast pops slide some butter over it
  5. Once the egg is looking tasty shovel that bad boy onto the toast
  6. Season with salt and pepper
