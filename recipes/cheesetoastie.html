Cheese Toastie

The staple of every lazy mans diet, the classic Cheese toastie!

Fast, easy and tasty. What more do you need!?

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Warm up toastie machine
  2. Butter the bread
  3. Sprinkle cheese everywhere
  4. Place other slice of bread on top and pop in the toastie machine
  5. Pat yourself on the back for a job well done!
